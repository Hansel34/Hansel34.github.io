title: The Perfect Steak
---

Ingredients: 

- A big fat steak from any store - I personally love Costco (NY Strip, Ribeye, Tenderloin)
- Salt & Pepper
- An empty stomach

Instructions:
1. Heat up a cast iron pan.
2. Wait until smoking
3. Slap steak on
4. 5 minutes~ each side. 
5. Season generously

DELICIOUS
